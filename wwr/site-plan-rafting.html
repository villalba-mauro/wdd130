Purpose

Booking and Reservation: An online presence enables customers to easily browse available trips, check schedules, and make reservations or bookings directly through the website.
This streamlines the process for customers and can increase booking rates.
Information and Education: Providing detailed information about the rafting trips, including difficulty levels, safety measures, required equipment, and frequently asked questions,
helps potential customers feel informed and confident in their decision to book a trip.
